The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name:

Magdalena del Mar Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The Magdalena del Mar Chamber of Commerce acts as a local economic catalyst, promoter of innovation and community advocate.

We promote business through member services, economic development and creating public policies that encourage new investments.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber host this month that promote business networking?
  • What are the job positions with the greatest demand in local companies?
  • What are the characteristics of the economically active public in the city?
  • How is the behavior of the supply and demand indices of goods and services in the locality?
